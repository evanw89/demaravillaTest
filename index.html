About Me

With a background in Kinesiology and Holistic Health, as well as 13 years of experience and extensive training, my practice focuses on Therapeutic Body Work and Massage Therapy. Originally trained in Esalen style massage, my technique has evolved into a fusion of Eastern and Western methods learned from exceptional teachers and mentors. These methods have trained me to focus on soothing and subtle bodywork, Deep Tissue work, Trigger Point Therapy and the extremely beneficial Active Release Technique (ART).

I have worked with a variety of clients, from individuals who are bed-ridden and are looking for a gentle, restoring massage, to elite athletes seeking relief from pain and injuries and in need of specially targeted treatment (such as Active Release Technique). Every massage allows me to get in touch with the client's body and address not only the healing process of the body but also the mind. My practice is a warm and welcoming environment where clients can shed everyday stresses to relax and reconnect in the face of restoration and rejuvenation.
